Liger Investments

Liger Trading NZ (established in 2019) is part of an established global organisation (founded in 2007), operating on the cutting-edge of electronic trading on the world’s largest exchanges.

Liger Holdings

Within the Liger portfolio, we have a strong investment arm with Liger Holdings.

Our primary mission is to invest in local ventures with positive fundamentals, extraordinary teams, and growth potential.

Liger Management Group

Liger Management Group is a proprietary firm specialising in professional advisory services exclusively to our Liger portfolio of businesses. Directed by entrepreneurs, based out of Queenstown, our vision is to build and support local through the company that we keep. Big ideas, aptly matched with the optimistic energy, enthusiasm and desire to contribute.

We provide leadership and strategic guidance, accounting and financial modelling and market analysis to Liger’s commercial and not-for-profit endeavours.
