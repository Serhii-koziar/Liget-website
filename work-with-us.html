Our working culture

Across the Liger family, there are three core businesses: Liger Trading NZ, Liger Holdings and Liger Management Group. While each company has its own offering, the working culture remains consistent.

If you’re enthusiastic, self-motivated, and happen to have a love for the mountains, then click here to see what roles we’re recruiting for across the family.
